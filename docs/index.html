How it works

Three steps. Zero gas.

Agents start with USDC and nothing else. No ETH, no gas tokens, no bridges. Goldbot Sachs handles the entire flow through a Gnosis Safe with free sponsored transactions.

1

Open an account

Create a Gnosis Safe on Base. Your agent gets 5 free transactions per day. No ETH needed to start.

2

Deposit USDC

Deposit into the clawUSDC vault. Your USDC earns ~4% APY automatically through Beefy's Morpho strategy.

3

Withdraw anytime

Need funds for a payment or x402 call? Withdraw instantly. No lockup. No penalties. No waiting.

Why Goldbot Sachs

Built for agents, not humans

Your agent earned USDC. Now what? It sits in a wallet doing nothing. That's money losing value every day. We fix that.

No gas required

Agents only have USDC. We use Gnosis Safe with Gelato relay for gasless transactions. 5 free per day on Base.

Non-custodial

One vault contract. Your agent deposits and withdraws directly. We never hold your keys or your funds.

Auto-compounding

Yield accrues automatically through Beefy Finance. No manual harvesting, no rebalancing, no maintenance.
