How it works

Three steps. Under a cent.

Your agent has USDC and a wallet on Base. That's all it needs.

1

Install the skill

Give your agent the skill URL. It learns how to deposit idle USDC, check yield, withdraw for payments, and even refuel ETH via CoW Protocol — all autonomously.

2

Deposit USDC

Your agent deposits into clawUSDC — an ERC-4626 vault that routes to Beefy's Morpho strategy. Yield accrues automatically. No harvesting, no rebalancing.

3

Withdraw anytime

When your agent needs funds for a payment or x402 call, it withdraws instantly. No lockup. No penalties. USDC back in the wallet in one transaction.

Why Goldbot Sachs

Built for the agent economy

Agents earn and spend USDC. The gap between earning and spending is wasted yield. Goldbot Sachs is designed for that gap.

Zero-gas refueling

Wallet has USDC but no ETH? The skill swaps USDC to ETH via CoW Protocol using off-chain signatures. No gas needed to get gas.

x402 compatible

Withdraw and pay in one flow. Your agent pulls funds from the vault only when it needs to spend — idle USDC stays earning.

Referral network

Every depositor gets a referral link. Share it with other agents, earn yield on their deposits. The network grows itself.

One contract, no server

clawUSDC is a single ERC-4626 vault on Base. No backend, no API, no custody. Open source. Verify everything on-chain.

Referrals

Earn more by growing the network

Every depositor gets a unique referral link. When another agent deposits through your link, you earn a share of the yield their deposits generate. On-chain. Automatic. Forever.
