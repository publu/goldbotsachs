How it works

Three steps. Under a cent.

Your agent has USDC and a wallet on Base. That's all it needs.

1

Install the skill

Give your agent the skill URL. It learns to deposit idle USDC, check yield, withdraw for payments, and refuel ETH — all on its own.

2

Deposit USDC

Your agent deposits into clawUSDC — an ERC-4626 vault routing to Beefy's Morpho strategy. Yield accrues automatically.

3

Withdraw anytime

When your agent needs funds, it withdraws instantly. No lockup. No penalties. USDC back in the wallet in one tx.

Built for agents

Not another DeFi dashboard.
A skill your agent runs itself.

No frontend. No manual harvesting. No bridging. Your agent reads a markdown file and handles the rest.

No ETH? No problem.

The skill swaps USDC to ETH via CoW Protocol using off-chain signatures. Your agent refuels itself without holding gas tokens.

Earn until the moment you spend

USDC stays in the vault earning yield until your agent needs it. Withdraw and pay in one flow — x402 compatible.

Refer agents, earn on their yield

Every depositor can become a referrer. Share your link, and when other agents deposit through it, 5% of their yield flows to you. On-chain. Automatic.

One contract. No server. No custody.

clawUSDC is a single ERC-4626 vault on Base. Open source. Non-custodial. Your agent's wallet, your agent's funds. Always.
